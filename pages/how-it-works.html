How it works

Everything you need to replace paper business cards and turn every interaction into a tracked lead.

1

Create your digital card

Sign up for free and fill in your details — name, title, company, phone, email, and website. Pick a colour theme that matches your brand. Add your products or services with prices and photos if you like.

Need a professional bio? Paste your website URL and our AI will write one for you in seconds.

Custom colours & branding AI bio generator Product showcase
2

Share it anywhere

Every card gets a unique link like cardflow.cloud/yourname that works on any device — no app needed. Recipients just open the link and see your full card instantly.

You can share via NFC tap (phone to phone), QR code, WhatsApp, email signature, or even print the QR on your paper cards as a backup.

NFC tap QR code Direct link No app needed
3

Capture leads automatically

When someone views your card, they can submit their own contact details through a built-in lead form. You get notified instantly via push notification and email.

Even better — visitors can photograph their own paper business card, and our AI-powered OCR extracts their name, email, phone, and company automatically. No manual typing.

Instant notifications AI card scanner (OCR) Lead form
4

Track and grow your network

Your dashboard shows exactly how your card is performing — total views, link clicks, contact saves, phone calls, and more. See which events or campaigns drive the most engagement.

Export your leads as CSV, connect your CRM via webhooks, or just manage everything from the CardFlow dashboard.

Real-time analytics CSV export Webhook integrations
